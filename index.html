About

Hello, I'm Lola.

Welcome to my Site!

I am a Senior Software Engineer, working on the infrastructure that powers payments. I care deeply about building systems that are reliable, observable, and maintainable.

I have built robust and scalable systems for Moniepoint and was responsible for the engingeering infrastucture that supported our terminal-as-a-moat distribution strategy.

I'm originally from Lagos,Nigeria and recently moved to London. I find myself endlessly curious about how large-scale systems work, and I try to document what I learn and built so it's useful for others too.

This site is a place to think out loud — about engineering, career growth, books, and anything that tickles my fancy.
